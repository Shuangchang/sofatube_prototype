SOFA Screening 2016
SOFA Screening program
All students in SOFA who produce film and animation shorts during each semester are expected to provide those works to the school's end-of-semester screenings forum held during Finals Week. At this venue, student work is critiqued by faculty and peers. This process serves as a critical element for the students, aiding in the development of artistic expression and teaching interaction with an audience. The screening is free and open to public.
